We are Leading Company

NITHART MEDIA LTD is a specialized firm which business activities centre on printing and publishing in addition to trading, merchandising and general contract. The company also engages in marketing and advertising services. Amongst the firm's products are booklets, brochures, business cards, calendars, textbooks, envelopes, flyers and gift certificate. Others include greeting cards, invitations, letterhead, magnets and notepads, pet cards (special), postcards, posters, presentation Folders and rack cards. The company's headquarter is situated in Kano State the commercial city of northern Nigeria. The company was incorporated in June 2010. NITHART MEDIA LTD has a team of graphic artists, expert designers, veteran printers, professional binders & laminators and specialists finishers. The firm provides specialized quality printing and publishing services covering micro, small, medium and large format designs. The core values of the company are hard work, trust, integrity, excellence, commitment, professionalism and customer satisfaction. NITHART MEDIA LTD communicates on behalf of its clients and their customers with the goal of selling clients products and services. Communicating the value of our clients' product and services is core to our business objectives. We advertise and manage exchange relationships as well as deliver exchange offerings that have value for customers, clients, partners and society at large.

Objective

Our objectives include offering publishing services to individuals, private organizations and government establishments. Our services covers publication of books, monograms, catalogues, myth, fictions, kids' stories, theories and motivational books. We also trade in general merchandise including building equipments, household utensils, kitchen utensils, body and food products, etc. Our objectives encompass publishing seminar brochures, conference proceedings, journals and all types of books in all areas of human Endeavour at lowest cost and best quality. We provide forum for researchers, industrialists, professionals, engineers, managers, and policy makers working in various fields to knowledge to share information and exchange ideas. We partner with world class printers and publishers to delivers full blown colour separation with high resolution pictures and diagrams second to none. Our operations encompass high speed manuscript formatting and editing, text editing and proofreading before and after publication.
